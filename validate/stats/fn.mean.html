Calculates the mean of a dataset

The math

\bar{x} = \frac{\sum_{i=0}^{n}x_i}{n}

Example

use validate::stats::mean;
use validate::assert_close;

let x = vec![0.; 10];

assert_close!(0., mean(&x));

let x = vec![-1., -1., 1., 1.];
assert_close!(0., mean(&x));

Panics

  • If the dataset is empty
